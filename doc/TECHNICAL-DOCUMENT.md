    + Android 4.3+ (Samsung Galaxy S4/S5)

## Responsive approach
  - Breakpoints
    + Large screen: 1200 and above
    + Medium screen: 1024 to 1199
    + Small screen: 768 to 1023
    + Extra small screen: below 768
